# Horary LLM Requirements

## Project A: LLM Expertise & Skillset

The LLM needs to be an expert in traditional horary astrology. Below are the core competencies and knowledge areas it must have.

### 1. Traditional Planetary Rulerships

**Classical Dignity System:**
```
Sun: Rules Leo, Exalted in Aries (19°), Detriment in Aquarius, Fall in Libra (19°)
Moon: Rules Cancer, Exalted in Taurus (3°), Detriment in Capricorn, Fall in Scorpio (3°)
Mercury: Rules Gemini & Virgo, Exalted in Virgo (15°), Detriment in Sagittarius & Pisces, Fall in Pisces (15°)
Venus: Rules Taurus & Libra, Exalted in Pisces (27°), Detriment in Aries & Scorpio, Fall in Virgo (27°)
Mars: Rules Aries & Scorpio, Exalted in Capricorn (28°), Detriment in Libra & Taurus, Fall in Cancer (28°)
Jupiter: Rules Sagittarius & Pisces, Exalted in Cancer (15°), Detriment in Gemini & Virgo, Fall in Capricorn (15°)
Saturn: Rules Capricorn & Aquarius, Exalted in Libra (21°), Detriment in Cancer & Leo, Fall in Aries (21°)
```

**Dignity Scoring:**
- Domicile (Ruler): +5 points
- Exaltation: +4 points
- Triplicity: +3 points
- Term: +2 points
- Face: +1 point
- Peregrine (no dignity): 0 points
- Detriment: -4 points
- Fall: -5 points

### 3. Accidental Dignities

**House Placement:**
- Angular houses (1, 4, 7, 10): +5 points (strongest)
- Succedent houses (2, 5, 8, 11): +3 points (moderate)
- Cadent houses (3, 6, 9, 12): +1 point (weakest)

**Special House Positions:**
- Joy by house (e.g., Mercury in 1st, Moon in 3rd, Venus in 5th, Mars in 6th, Sun in 9th, Jupiter in 11th, Saturn in 12th)

**Aspect Quality:**
- Applying aspects to benefics (Jupiter, Venus): positive
- Separating from malefics (Mars, Saturn): positive
- Applying to malefics: challenging
- Separating from benefics: lost opportunity

**Planetary Speed:**
- Swift in motion (faster than average): +2 points
- Average speed: 0 points
- Slow/stationary: -2 points
- Retrograde: -5 points

**Light (Visibility):**
- Oriental (rises before Sun): benefic for planets except Moon
- Occidental (sets after Sun): benefic for Moon
- Under Sun's beams (within 17°): weakened (-4)
- Combust (within 8° of Sun): severely weakened (-5)
- Cazimi (within 17' of Sun): strengthened (+5)

### 4. Perfection of Aspects

**Traditional Aspects & Orbs:**
```
Conjunction: 0° (orb: ±8° for luminaries, ±6° for planets)
Sextile: 60° (orb: ±6° for luminaries, ±3-4° for planets)
Square: 90° (orb: ±8° for luminaries, ±6° for planets)
Trine: 120° (orb: ±8° for luminaries, ±6° for planets)
Opposition: 180° (orb: ±8° for luminaries, ±6° for planets)
```

**Aspect Application:**
- **Applying**: Faster planet moving toward exact aspect with slower planet → event WILL happen
- **Separating**: Faster planet moving away from exact aspect → event already happened or won't happen
- **Partile**: Aspect within 1° (most powerful)
- **Platic**: Aspect within traditional orb but not partile

### 5. Prohibitions & Frustrations

**Prohibition:**
- When an applying aspect is interrupted by a third planet making an aspect before perfection
- Indicates something/someone will prevent the outcome

**Frustration:**
- When a planet turns retrograde before perfection
- OR when a planet leaves the sign before perfection
- Indicates the matter will not come to pass

**Refranation:**
- When the faster planet turns retrograde before perfection
- The querent changes their mind or abandons the matter

### 6. Translation & Collection of Light

**Translation of Light:**
- A faster planet separating from one significator and applying to another
- Brings the two parties/matters together through a third party/means
- The translating planet describes the intermediary

**Collection of Light:**
- A slower planet receives aspects from both significators
- Brings parties together through a mutual connection/interest
- The collecting planet shows the common ground

### 7. Moon Considerations

**Void of Course Moon:**
- Moon has made her last major aspect in current sign
- Next aspect won't occur until she changes signs
- Traditional interpretation: "Nothing will come of the matter"
- Exception: Moon in Taurus, Cancer, Sagittarius, Pisces (still effective)

**Moon's Last Aspect:**
- Shows what the querent has recently experienced
- Context for the current situation

**Moon's Next Aspect:**
- Shows what will happen next
- Critical for timing and outcome

**Moon's Essential Dignity:**
- Strong Moon (dignified): querent has resources and support
- Weak Moon (debilitated): querent lacks power or information

### 8. Critical Degrees

**0° of any sign:**
- Too early to judge (immature situation)
- Insufficient information

**29° of any sign:**
- Too late to judge (matter already decided)
- Urgency or crisis
- Exception: If planet rules the sign, this can be "exalted position"

**15° fixed signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius):**
- Turning points, critical moments

### 9. Reception

**Reception Types:**
- **Mutual Reception by Dignity**: Two planets in each other's signs of dignity (powerful connection)
- **Reception by Exaltation**: Planet in sign of other's exaltation
- **Mixed Reception**: Various dignity combinations
- **No Reception**: Planets have no dignity relationship

**Interpretation:**
- Good reception: Parties are disposed favorably toward each other
- Bad reception (detriment/fall): Dislike, resistance, obstacles
- No reception: Indifference

### 10. Question Radicality (Is chart valid?)

**Considerations Before Judgment:**
1. Saturn in 1st or 7th house: Astrologer's judgment may be impaired
2. Moon in late Via Combusta (15° Libra to 15° Scorpio): Chart may be unreliable
3. Moon void of course (except in favored signs)
4. 0-3° rising: Too early to judge
5. 27-30° rising: Too late to judge
6. Moon in 3° applying to combustion

**When NOT to judge:**
- Question is not sincere or urgent
- Querent is testing the astrologer
- Question violates ethics (death predictions, harm to others)

### 11. House Significators by Question Type

**Relationship Questions:**
- 1st house: Querent
- 7th house: Partner/other person
- 5th house: Romance, new love
- 8th house: Partner's resources
- Moon: Co-significator of querent (always)
- Venus: Natural significator of love/relationships

**Career/Job Questions:**
- 1st house: Querent
- 10th house: Career, reputation, employer
- 2nd house: Income from the job
- 6th house: Daily work environment
- Sun: Natural significator of career (men), Moon (women)

**Lost Object Questions:**
- 1st house: Querent
- 2nd house: Lost object (if valuable/personal)
- Moon: Co-significator of object
- House ruler of sign on 2nd cusp: The object
- Aspects to Moon: Clues to location

**Property/Real Estate:**
- 4th house: Property, home, land
- 10th house: Seller/landlord
- 1st house: Buyer/querent

**Legal Matters:**
- 9th house: Courts, legal proceedings
- 7th house: Opponents in litigation
- 10th house: Judge

### 12. Timing Techniques

**By Sign Type:**
- Cardinal signs (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): Days or weeks
- Fixed signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): Months or years
- Mutable signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): Weeks or months

**By Orb:**
- 1° = 1 unit of time (scaled by sign type)
- If aspect is 3° from exact in cardinal sign: 3 days/weeks
- If aspect is 5° from exact in fixed sign: 5 months

**By House:**
- Angular houses: Quick (days to weeks)
- Succedent houses: Moderate (weeks to months)
- Cadent houses: Slow (months to years)

### 13. Planetary Speeds (Daily Motion)

**Average Daily Motion:**
```
Moon: ~13° per day (fastest)
Sun: ~1° per day
Mercury: 1-2° per day (varies widely, often retrograde)
Venus: 1-1.25° per day
Mars: 0.5° per day
Jupiter: 0.08-0.15° per day
Saturn: 0.03-0.08° per day (slowest)
```

**Interpretation:**
- Faster than average: Strong, active, effective
- Slower than average: Weak, passive, ineffective
- Retrograde: Contrary, backwards, internal
